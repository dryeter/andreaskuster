Andreas Kuster

Andreas is a Computer Science Doctoral Student at Nanyang Technological University in Singapore, supervised by Prof. Mo Li in the Wireless and Networked Distributed Sensing group. His research focuses on resilient wireless communication, from advanced spectrum sensing, interference detection and mitigation to intelligent jamming on the physical and message access control layer. He aims to pair machine learning, signal processing and low-level RTL approaches with state-of-the-art software defined radio platforms to build robust wireless communication systems.
